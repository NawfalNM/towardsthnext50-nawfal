  • The Role of Youth

    • Role in Sustainability

      In 2021, Youth constitute 25% of DEWA’s workforce and have made immeasurable efforts towards a sustainable future. Youth Centres like the Arab Youth Centre have established an Arab Youth Council for Climate Change.

    • Role in Government

      Shamma Al Mazurui was appointed the UAE’s Minister of State for Youth Affairs at the age of 22, making her the world’s youngest government minister. With her, the role of Youth in the government is projected to increase in the future to have more youth representation.

    • Role in Computer Science

      The UAE government recognizes the importance of computer science and automation for its future and as such has launched many initiatives to help its youth enter the industry. Initiatives like the GITEX YouthX will help the youth enter the computer science space fully prepared.
